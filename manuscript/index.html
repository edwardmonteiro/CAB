English Verb Tenses

Simple Present

Examples:

  • I eat breakfast every day.
  • She works in an office.

Rules and Tips:

  • Used to describe actions that are happening now or that happen regularly.
  • Affirmative: Subject + base form of verb.
  • Negative: Subject + do/does not + base form of verb.
  • Interrogative: Do/does + subject + base form of verb?

Present Continuous

Examples:

  • I am eating lunch right now.
  • They are watching a movie at home.

Rules and Tips:

  • Used to describe actions that are happening now and will continue for a period of time.
  • Form: Subject + be verb (am, is, are) + present partic
